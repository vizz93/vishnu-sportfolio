In a nutshell

So, what's this all about?

Imagine you and your squad are out living your best lives, but when it comes to tracking how much everyone’s spending, things get a little wild. Who paid for that last dinner? Who’s been splurging on weekend trips? Well, this app has your back! It’s like having a personal accountant, but without the boring spreadsheets and awkward "I owe you" convos. It lets you and your crew set a budget, track your expenses in real time, and keep everything in check—so you can spend more time having fun and less time doing math. No more "I’ll figure it out later" moments—just simple, seamless tracking for everyone.

The Problem

Here’s the thing: when you’re out with a group, tracking all those little expenses can quickly become a hot mess. You’re each paying from your own accounts, but the budget is still a shared thing. How do you figure out who’s been spending too much on snacks, or how much is left for the weekend getaway? And, what if someone buys something personal and doesn’t want it to mess up the group’s budget? Keeping track of everything without it turning into a giant headache is the real struggle.

The Solution

Enter the app! It keeps track of everyone’s spending in the background, updates in real time, and even generates easy-to-read reports showing where the money’s going. It’s like a financial detective—without the boring part. You can even mark personal expenses so they don’t mess with the group’s totals. No more confusion, no more "who’s turn is it to pay," just clear, smooth tracking so you can focus on enjoying the moment. Let the app handle the numbers—your crew will thank you!
