An idea that might delight

Sooo.... A few Days ago on a mid night i was travelling towards my home and i asked my friend to pick me up from the bus station, though he was sleepy he agreed to pick up after i promised him to get the famous jigarthanda for him, all he asked was to wake him up when i reach "melur" which is about 20kms from the bus station so that he can reach the station on time.



Now, I am sleepy and not a ray of sunlight outside, i dont know how i can make a call to my friend on the agreed location

Should i stay awake and watch my location on the map?

Should i ask the driver to tell me when i reach mellur?

Confused me got an idea i stated navigation towards melur and found the estimated time to arrive and set an timer to that time

Why should it has to be that way!?

I Opened up my keep notes and took note of the incident and went to sleep and somehow i managed to miss melur and ended up waking up too close the station, i didnt want to trouble my friend and wait till he arrived! I just took a rapido!


BTW i got him the jigarthanda!



Two days late i got some time and i looked into this problem and thought of solving it! and off i went to Figma! and hit "F"

I introduced a new option called "Alert" right next to Share

Which will open up to a list of options which will alert you based on the option you select

from there you can take things up your hand!





Now i can sleep in peace!
